Pass the 💣 - Tutorial

Join or create a game

To play a game you can either create a new one or join an existing game.

How the game works

At the beginning a 💣 is randomly distributed to one of the players.
While possessing the 💣 it loses life points. By tapping on the 💣 it can be further made angry.
This is also the way for you to gain points and outreach your enemies.

But beware! The initial amount of life points is unknown.
If unsure, better pass the 💣 to one of your enemies. Drag and Hold to move it around. Better be quick, because as long as you have the 💣, you 'll never know how hot it is.

End of Round

When the 💣 eplodes winners gain extra points and one poor soul loses them all. After one player reaches above 100 points. He is the ultimate winner of the game.
